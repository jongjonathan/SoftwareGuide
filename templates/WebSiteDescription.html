About

My website will be about my life as a Concordia student in the Software Engineering program in the form of a blog. It will be like a personal blog where I will be able to create a guide for a first year Software Engineering Student and what they should expect. The guide will try to go into details about how I found my first semester and what I have learned to become a better student and what I could have done to improve my first semester. It will focus on my everyday studying and work as well as include many personal aspects of my life. It will consist of how I balance my personal and student life. I will try to organize my student life and personal life in different tabs on the website. This may be divided into smaller sections with my interests and strategies for studying and tips for keeping a healthy lifestyle. There will also be a section for where I can post my daily tasks and what I have done for that day and how my first semester was like.

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to inform any new students about the experience of a first year SOEN student and the transition from CEGEP to university. In the future, if it is possible, I will try to have a section for questions, or even Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS) to supply answers to any possible questions. The end goal is to simplify the reader's life of entering the program with what to expect and the do's and the don'ts of starting university. This is coming from personal experience since I had wished that there was a guide to help me understand what to do first. Transitioning from CEGEP was quite challenging and I want any SOEN student to feel at ease when being part of that transition.
